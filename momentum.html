⚡ Momentum

A 21-Day Challenge for Personal Growth

🎯 What is Momentum?

Momentum is a 21-day accountability challenge where friends commit to daily goals and track their progress. It's designed to push you to be your best self through friendly competition, mutual support, and consistent daily wins.

📋 How It Works

  • Daily Posts: Share what you accomplished each day (fitness, learning, work, reading, etc.)
  • Scoring: Earn 0-20 points per day based on the quality and detail of your accomplishments
  • Penalties: Miss a day? Lose 10 points
  • Elimination: Score reaches 0? You're out (but can still watch)
  • 21 Days: Complete the full challenge to compete for the trophy

🏆 The Winner

The member with the highest cumulative score after 21 days wins! Consistency, quality efforts, and dedication to your goals are what matter most.

🎁 Badges & Perks

  • 🐦 Early Bird: First to post each day
  • 🔥 Consistent: 7-day posting streak
  • 🌟 Overachiever: Perfect 20-point day
  • 💪 Comeback Kid: Recover from low score to 50+

✨ Why Join?

Challenge yourself, stay accountable to friends, and build better habits in 21 days. Whether it's fitness, learning, work projects, or personal goals—this is your moment to build momentum and make real progress.
